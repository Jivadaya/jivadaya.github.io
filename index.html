Welcome to ISKCON PUNE SANKIRTAN

Spreading Spiritual Wisdom to Every Heart

Jivadaya ISKCON Pune is committed to sharing the transformative teachings of Srila Prabhupada by distributing spiritual books across communities. Through our Sankirtan bus party, we reach villages, bringing these life-changing books to rural areas. In schools, we conduct Gita Contests to inspire young minds. Our mission is to reach every individual, igniting a journey of self-discovery and spiritual awakening. With your support, we can fulfill this vision. Join us in spreading wisdom and compassion—please help us make a difference.

Donations

1. Donations made to ISKCON are non-refundable

  • All donations made to ISKCON are voluntary and non-refundable.
  • ISKCON reserves the right to allocate donated funds to any of its ongoing or future charitable, spiritual, or educational activities.

2. Non-Refundable Policy

  • Donations once made cannot be cancelled, refunded, or transferred, regardless of the mode of payment.
  • Donors are advised to double-check the amount, purpose, and personal information before making the donation.

3. Donation Receipts & 80G Certificate

  • Donors will receive a receipt for every successful donation via email or WhatsApp (if details are provided).
  • Donations are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, where applicable.
  • 80G certificates will be issued for donations above ₹5000/-  within 7–10 working days. Please ensure correct PAN and contact details are provided for this.
  • All 10BE certificates for 80G donation made to Jivadaya ISKCON Pune can be downloaded from the link https://certificatemis.encureit.com

4. Use of Funds

  • ISKCON commits to using all donations responsibly for spiritual, humanitarian, and educational services such as:
    • Book distribution
    • Temple maintenance
    • Food relief programs (prasadam distribution)
    • Festivals and public outreach events
    • Education and youth development
