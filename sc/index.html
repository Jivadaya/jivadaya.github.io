Pitru Paksha Mahadaan

Donate Srimad Bhagavatam for the Liberation of Your Ancestors.

`Pitru Paksha` is a sacred period to honor our departed ancestors and offer them prayers for their peace and spiritual journey. On this occasion, we invite you to participate in a profound act of charity (`Daan`) by supporting the distribution of sacred scriptures.

The Power of `Srimad Bhagavatam` Daan

The donation of sacred wisdom is considered the highest form of charity. By supporting the printing and distribution of `Srimad Bhagavatam`, you are giving the divine gift of eternal knowledge. This selfless act purifies the hearts of both the donor and the recipient, and it grants immense spiritual benefit to the departed souls, helping them on their journey to the spiritual world.

Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam is the transcendental science not only for knowing the ultimate source of everything but also for knowing our relation with Him and our duty toward perfection of the human society on the basis of this perfect knowledge.

Lord Krishna blesses our ancestors when we read, hear and glorify Him. The Srimad Bhagavatam is the essence of all vedic scriptures because it is full of pastimes of Lord Krishna.

This Śrīmad-Bhāgavatam is the literary incarnation of God. It is meant for the ultimate good of all people, and it is all-successful, all-blissful and all-perfect. - Srimad-Bhagavatam 1.3.40

Donate or Gift Srimad Bhagavatam to devotees, to your friends, family members, colleagues, neighbours or anyone and offer Lord Krishna's blessings on them. Your ancestors will surely be happy with this act of charity.

There is an additional delivery charge of ₹800 per set if you want Srimad Bhagavatam to be delivered at your postal address.

  • Donations once made cannot be cancelled, refunded, or transferred, regardless of the mode of payment.
  • Donors are advised to double-check the amount, purpose, and personal information before making the donation.

  • Donors will receive a receipt for every successful donation via email or WhatsApp (if details are provided).
  • Donations are eligible for tax exemption under Section 80G of the Income Tax Act, where applicable.
  • 80G certificates will be issued for donations above ₹5000/- within 7–10 working days. Please ensure correct PAN and contact details are provided for this.
  • All 10BE certificates for 80G donation made to Jivadaya ISKCON Pune can be downloaded from the link https://certificatemis.encureit.com

4. Use of Funds

  • ISKCON commits to using all donations responsibly for spiritual, humanitarian, and educational services such as:

    • Temple maintenance
    • Food relief programs (prasadam distribution)
    • Festivals and public outreach events
    • Education and youth development
